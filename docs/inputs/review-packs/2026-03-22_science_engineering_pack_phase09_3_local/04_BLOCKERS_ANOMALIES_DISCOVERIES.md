# Blockers, Anomalies, And Discoveries

## Main Blockers

- The packet still does not route selectively enough on the governing broad surface.
- The packet also does not route selectively enough on the current best owned narrow surveillance surface.

## Anomalies

- Some event-annotated surveillance clips are event-saturated and therefore poor triage surfaces.
- Even within the sparse facility cohort, clip behavior is not consistent.
- Event-boundary windows were not universally stronger than event-active windows in exploratory local probes.

## Discoveries

- The best currently owned narrow evaluator is structured facility surveillance, not generic surveillance and not general video.
- The Mac can execute the `09.3` narrow-surface runner locally in the arm64 lab environment.
- The current packet delta contains real localized signal, but not stable enough signal to support a wedge claim.

## What These Mean

The next move should not be “more of the same with smaller threshold steps.” The current blocker is not lack of one more scorer. It is that the artifact-evaluator pairing is still too weak and too heterogeneous.
