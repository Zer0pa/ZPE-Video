# Code of Conduct

## Standard

Contributors must keep discussion evidence-first, specific, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Treat negative findings as useful.
- Cite exact files, commands, and outputs when challenging a claim.
- Keep critique focused on the work and its evidence.
- Respect private security and licensing channels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Fabricating evidence or suppressing adverse results.
- Posting private credentials, private security details, or confidential artifacts in public channels.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns to `architects@zer0pa.ai`. Maintainers may close issues, remove comments, or restrict participation when behavior undermines evidence-based review.
