## Brief

The Seedance-informed factorized bundle beats the flat lane (0.6866x ratio)
but loses to the simpler temporal-only split (1.7571x ratio). The persistence
carrier has not earned its complexity. The hypothesis that subchannel
factorization provides a wedge over flat alternatives is not confirmed on
this surface. The temporal-only split remains the dominant baseline.

## Note on Enum Selection

The `INCONCLUSIVE` mapping (rather than `FAIL`) reflects the fact that real
factorization signal exists vs the flat baseline (0.6866x vs 1.0x is real
compression), even though the wedge hypothesis was falsified by the simpler
temporal-only baseline. A stricter reading would map this to `FAIL` because
the hypothesis under test was the wedge, not the existence of any signal at
all. The `INCONCLUSIVE` label is retained because the underlying signal is
still useful as a building block for combined approaches and was not pure
noise; falsification was specifically of the wedge claim, not of the
factorization itself.
